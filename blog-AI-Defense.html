Systematic AI Response Cycle

STEP 01

Ingest & Normalize

AI ingests TBs of logs from firewalls, clouds, and endpoints, normalizing data formats for analysis.

STEP 02

AI Inference

Machine Learning models compare live traffic against baseline behavior to identify anomalies (Zero-Day).

STEP 03

Automated Triage

AI correlates alerts into a single 'Incident', reducing alert fatigue by filtering 99% of false positives.

STEP 04

Autonomous Action

Pre-approved playbooks execute immediately: blocking IPs, killing processes, or resetting passwords.
